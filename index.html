Wordle, but it lies to you.

Six guesses to find the five-letter word. The colour feedback follows a hidden, deterministic schedule of lies. Solve the word and figure out the bluff.

How to play Bluff

Guess the five-letter word in six tries. After each guess the tiles recolour:

A in the word and in the right place
B in the word but somewhere else
C not in the word

The catch. The colours lie. Every puzzle has a hidden lie schedule — some tiles will report the wrong colour on purpose, in a deterministic pattern. Same lie all day, same puzzle for everyone. You have to solve the word and reverse- engineer the lie at the same time.

A daily puzzle. New word every day at midnight local.
